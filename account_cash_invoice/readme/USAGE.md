1.  Go to *Settings* and activate the developer mode.
2.  Go to *Settings / Users & Companies / Users* and set the flag 'Show
    Full Accounting Features'.
3.  Go to *Invoicing / Dashboard* from a Cash Journal.
4.  On the Additional options, Press the button **Pay Invoice** to pay a
    Supplier a Customer Invoice/Refund.
5.  Select the invoice type and select invoice to pay.
6.  Press **Pay invoice** on the statement. The payment will then be
    reconciled with the invoice.
